Our Services

We focus on industrial utilities, waste heat and DSM to turn existing assets into decarbonization levers.

Utilities & Energy Baseline

Structured baseline for gas, electricity and water: from quick diagnostic to detailed breakdown of loads, tariffs and savings potential.

Operational Inefficiencies

Identify processes that don’t need to run and hidden losses in ancillary systems such as compressed air and industrial gases, pumps and fans.

Waste Heat & Recovery

Map and quantify waste heat across your site, prioritize recovery options and assess business cases for heat pumps, ORC and other retrofit solutions.

DSM & Flexible Tariffs

Turn flexible loads into assets: DSM / demand response strategies, alignment with flexible power tariffs and integration of on-site flexibility (e.g. PSA, storage).
